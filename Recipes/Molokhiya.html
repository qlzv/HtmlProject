    Ingredients

    • 1 kilogram of chicken, cut into quarters
    • 1 medium onion, quartered
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 5 cardamom pods
    • 2 bay leaves
    • 1 tablespoon of salt
    • 1 kilogram of chopped molokhia (fresh or frozen)
    • 10 garlic cloves, mashed
    • 1 tablespoon of ground coriander

    Note:

    Molokhia can also be prepared with other meats like lamb shanks, rabbit, or any preferred protein depending on taste and availability.
