Ingredients

  • For boiling the meat:
  • 2 kilograms of bone-in lamb, cut into large pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 10 cardamom pods
  • 10 black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon of mixed spices or Mansaf spice blend
  • 2 tablespoons of salt
  • For cooking the rice:
  • 1/4 cup of ghee
  • 3 cups of Basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 4 1/2 cups of water
  • For cooking the yogurt:
  • 1 1/2 kilograms of yogurt (can be a mix of cow's milk yogurt and other types)
  • 2 cups of buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon of mixed spices or Mansaf spice blend
